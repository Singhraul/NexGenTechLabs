FAQ

Quick Answers

How long does a project take?

+
MVPs take 2–4 weeks; full SaaS or marketplaces run 8–16 weeks. You get a precise milestone-based timeline after discovery.

Do you sign NDAs?

+
Yes — always before any detailed discussion. We use your template or provide our own. Your ideas stay completely confidential.

Who owns the source code?

+
You do — 100%. On final payment, all code, docs, credentials, and configs transfer to you. No strings attached.

What's the payment structure?

+
Project-based: 40% upfront / 40% mid / 20% on delivery. Retainers: monthly in advance. We accept Razorpay, PayPal, Wise, bank transfer, and crypto.

Can you join my existing project?

+
Absolutely. We do code audits and collaborate with in-house teams. We adapt to your stack and tools — GitHub, Jira, Notion, Slack.

Do you provide post-launch support?

+
Every project includes 30-day free post-launch support. After that, retainer plans start at $4K/month with priority response times.
